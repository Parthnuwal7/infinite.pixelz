Hello, I'm Parth

Photographer | Visual Storyteller

Photography found me before I fully understood what it was. What began as a fascination with a simple camera evolved into an ongoing pursuit to capture emotion, detail and depth through still frames.

My Journey

I started photographing with a basic point-and-shoot camera when I was 13. That summer, I clicked blurry images, tilted skies and awkward portraits — but I was mesmerized by the idea that moments could be paused forever.

Years later, during the 2020 lockdown, I picked up a DSLR and started documenting what I saw — from everyday corners to fading light. With no formal training, I taught myself through constant trial, error and reflection. Photography soon became a language I spoke more fluently than words.

Today, I focus on street, portrait and conceptual photography. My style merges raw emotion with thoughtful composition, aiming to tell stories that linger — visually and emotionally.

My Approach

Authentic Moments

I believe the most powerful images are unposed — captured in between laughter, stillness, or motion. My process centers around comfort, trust and spontaneity.

Natural Light

I work predominantly with natural light — soft, unpredictable and beautifully real. It brings honesty to every frame and depth to every shadow.

Intentional Storytelling

Every shoot has a purpose. Whether personal or commercial, I approach each session with a clear narrative in mind — and ensure it unfolds authentically through my lens.

Services

Portrait Sessions

Individual and couple portraits with a focus on mood, emotion and story-driven compositions.

Event Coverage

Documenting events, college fests or curated gatherings with journalistic precision and creative flair.

Conceptual Projects

Commissioned conceptual shoots for brands, artists, or editorial work — built from moodboards to final frame.
